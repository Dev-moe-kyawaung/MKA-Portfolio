Senior Android Developer

Common Questions

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What technologies do you specialize in?
I specialize in Kotlin for Android development, with expertise in Jetpack Compose, MVVM architecture, Room database, Coroutines, Flow, Firebase, Retrofit, and Dagger/Hilt. I also work with Python, TensorFlow Lite for ML integration, and various CI/CD tools.
What is your development process?
My process includes: 1) Requirements gathering and analysis, 2) UI/UX design and approval, 3) Development with regular updates, 4) Testing and QA, 5) Deployment to Play Store, 6) Post-launch support and maintenance. I use agile methodology with weekly sprints.
How long does it take to build an app?
Timeline depends on complexity. A simple app takes 2-4 weeks, a medium-complexity app takes 6-8 weeks, and a complex enterprise app can take 3-6 months. I provide detailed timeline estimates after understanding your requirements.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes, all packages include post-launch support ranging from 1-3 months depending on the plan. This includes bug fixes, minor updates, and technical support. Extended support and maintenance contracts are also available.
Can you work with existing codebases?
Absolutely! I have extensive experience refactoring legacy code, migrating from Java to Kotlin, implementing new features in existing apps, and optimizing performance. I can conduct a code audit and provide recommendations.
What about app security and data privacy?
Security is a top priority. I implement encryption, secure authentication, data validation, secure network communication, and follow OWASP mobile security guidelines. I also ensure GDPR compliance and can conduct security audits.
